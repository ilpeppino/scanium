Privacy-first by design

Scanium processes camera frames on-device and discards them immediately after analysis. Cloud classification is optional and only runs when you enable it.

  • Scanned items and drafts stay on your device until you delete them.
  • On-device analysis is the default; no cloud calls required.
  • Optional cloud classification sends cropped images transiently and discards them after processing.
  • You can disable cloud classification and clear app storage at any time.
